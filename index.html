香港創業實驗室：全港第一個專為專上學生和畢業生而設的創業啟蒙訓練營，將會於 2013 年 6 月 1-8 日舉行。活動由香港互聯網協會、數碼港和香港科技園公司聯合主辦，並得到 20 多個院校和組織支持。

StartLab Hong Kong: The First Startup Bootcamp for Tertiary Students & Graduates in Hong Kong! It is going to be held from 1st to 8th June 2012 hosted by Internet Society Hong Kong, Cyberport and Hong Kong Science and Technology Parks Corporation and supported by nearly 20 institutions and organisations now.

合辦機構 Co-organizers

  • Internet Society Hong Kong
    香港互聯網協會
  • Hong Kong Cyberport Management Company Limited
    香港數碼港管理有限公司
  • Hong Kong Science and Technology Parks Corporation
    香港科技園公司
